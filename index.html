About Me

About Me

    I'm Zeal, a Research Associate at Penn Medicine. I graduated Drexel Uiversity with a B.S. in Biomedical Engineering and a Bioinformatics concentration in 2022. My current research focuses on how electronic health records and biobank data can be leveraged to understand the the complex phenotypic and genotypic interactions underlying pain intensity, substance use disorders and psychiatric disorders.

    During my early scientific training,

    In learning about population genetics and biobanking, I've become interested in how data flows from clinic practice to scientific labs through EHR systems and think about the utility of establishing similar frameworks in developing countries. Outside of science, I like hiking, backpacking and playing squash!
